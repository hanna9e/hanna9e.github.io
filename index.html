Recipe #1

  • Time: 20mins

Meat 2 pounds Local

Recipe#2

  • Time:1 hour
  • Serves:1
Ingredients Quantity Source
Pasta One pack Italian
Marinara sauce 2 cups Home made
Cheese 40 grams Parmesan

Step 1: Boil pasta for 10 mins, prepare sauce separately in a pot.
Step 2: Layer pasta then sauce, then cheese into a baking pan.
Step 3: Preheat oven to 400 and cook for 30 mins

Recipe#3

  • Time:15mins
  • Serves:3

Ingredient Quantity Source
Bacon half pound Applewood
Tatertots 600 grams Frozen
Cheese 2 packs Pepperjack

Step 1: Prepare tater tots by baking them for 10 mins.
Step 2: Put tater tots, bacon and cheese together in baking pan.
STep 3: Bake for 40 mins in 500 degree oven and let cool.
